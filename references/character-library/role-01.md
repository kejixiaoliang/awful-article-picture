# role-01: Xiaoliang

Default narrator role based on the bundled PNG examples. These PNG files are the canonical reference for role-01; do not replace them with simplified SVG sketches unless the user explicitly asks.

## Identity

Xiaoliang, an article narrator / author avatar who appears as a slightly panicked creator trying to understand fast-moving AI tools, workflows, releases, and messy technical ideas.

## Canonical Reference Images

Use these bundled PNG images when role-01 is selected:

- `assets/characters/role-01/xiaoliang-reference-01.png`
- `assets/characters/role-01/xiaoliang-reference-02.png`
- `assets/characters/role-01/xiaoliang-reference-03.png`

Treat these images as the source of truth for the character's look. They are not decorative examples.

## Visual Markers

- Black messy spiky hair, drawn as chunky uneven black shapes.
- Round white eyes with black pupils, often looking sideways or surprised.
- Simple tan face, small ears, small nose, red open mouth when speaking or startled.
- Blue long-sleeve shirt or blue top as the most important outfit marker.
- Gray pants and simple brown shoes when the full body appears.
- Thick rough black outline, slightly wobbly MS Paint / mouse-drawn contour.
- Simple cartoon proportions: oversized head, short body, awkward hands, stiff pose.
- Usually surrounded by article props: laptop, notebook, calendar, boxes, machines, tools, posters, papers, or Chinese short labels.

## Personality

- Curious, rushed, and overwhelmed.
- Looks like they are trying to keep up with AI updates.
- Often writing notes, holding papers, inspecting machines, stepping around problems, or reacting to new tools.
- Funny and readable, but still rough and low-quality, not a polished mascot.

## Usage

- Use this role only when the user selects `role-01`, says "角色1", or asks for the Xiaoliang narrator.
- Include the PNG reference paths in prompts whenever possible.
- Preserve the blue shirt, black spiky hair, round eyes, tan face, and rough black outline.
- Do not add glasses, a black hoodie, a dark jacket, or a different adult-tech-blogger design unless the user updates the role.
- Do not force role-01 into every image; use them when the narrator helps the article beat.
- Keep the character in the same article-illustration style as the reference PNGs: white background, thick uneven lines, colored props, crude perspective, and short Chinese labels only when needed.
- Do not trace the PNGs exactly. Reuse identity markers while changing pose, expression, props, and scene for the article.
