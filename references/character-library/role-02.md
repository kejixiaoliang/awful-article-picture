# role-02: Axu / 阿序

Second narrator role based on the bundled Axu PNG reference. Use this role for AI product exploration, tool testing, product observation, workflow notes, and personal publishing where the narrator should feel quieter and more observant than role-01.

## Identity

Axu / 阿序, an AI product manager and AI explorer. He tests new AI products, observes product details, records discoveries, and shares useful findings from the AI world.

## Canonical Reference Images

Use these bundled PNG images when role-02 is selected:

- `assets/characters/role-02/axu-reference-01.png`

Treat this image as the source of truth for the character's identity. It is a character reference, not a style target for polished final images.

## Visual Markers

- Messy short black hair, slightly soft and student-like.
- Round glasses with clear circular lenses.
- Calm tan face, small nose and mouth, often curious, focused, confused, or quietly surprised.
- Dark navy hoodie with white drawstrings as the most important outfit marker.
- Gray pants and white shoes when the full body appears.
- Slightly reserved posture: standing with hands in pockets, sitting behind a laptop, holding a notebook, reading, or thinking.
- Common props: laptop, "探索日志" notebook, pen, backpack, coffee cup, magnifier, light bulb, star sticker, small notes, product cards, or AI tool boxes.
- Color palette markers from the reference: dark navy `#1C2A39`, gray `#6B7280`, warm white `#E6E6E6`, pale yellow `#FFE58F`, soft blue `#A7C7E7`.

## Personality

- Curious, focused, sincere, and relaxed.
- Likes exploring new AI tools and recording what works.
- Feels like a nearby friend who shares discoveries rather than a loud presenter.
- Often reacts with quiet surprise, careful thinking, or "I found something interesting today" energy.

## Usage

- Use this role only when the user selects `role-02`, says "角色2", says "阿序", or asks for the Axu narrator.
- Include the PNG reference path in prompts whenever possible.
- Preserve the round glasses, dark navy hoodie with white drawstrings, messy black hair, calm curious expression, and exploration props.
- Do not change Axu into role-01 Xiaoliang: no bright blue shirt, no non-glasses face, no exaggerated chased-by-AI expression as the default identity.
- Do not force role-02 into every image; use them when the narrator helps the article beat.
- Keep the final image in the awful article-picture style: white background, thick uneven mouse-drawn lines, rough proportions, crude article props, and failed-redraw awkwardness.
- Do not copy the clean character sheet style from the reference. The final output should look like someone badly redrew Axu into an article scene in MS Paint.
- Do not trace the PNG exactly. Reuse identity markers while changing pose, expression, props, and scene for the article.
